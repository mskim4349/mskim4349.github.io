Part of the **2026 소재HUB 지정공모형-01 consortium**, in collaboration with national
research partners. UNIST's role is to develop AI-driven models linking the manufacturing
process of fiber-reinforced composites to their effective mechanical response, enabling
inverse design of lightweight components for next-generation rehabilitation robotics.

**My contribution.** Surrogate models for the process → microstructure → effective
properties chain, with an emphasis on uncertainty quantification so that downstream
design loops can reason about manufacturing variability rather than ignore it.

**Status.** Proposal phase — submission deadline **2026-05-11**.
