Professional Summary

Postdoctoral researcher at UNIST working at the boundary between computational solid mechanics and machine learning. Research focuses on neural-network constitutive modeling, indentation-based parameter identification, damage and fracture in cryogenic environments, and graph-neural-network surrogates for multi-physics simulation.
